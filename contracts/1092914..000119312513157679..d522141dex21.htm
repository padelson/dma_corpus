PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

WHEREAS, Seller owns all the outstanding member and other ownership interests of the Company (the “Member Interests”); and

WHEREAS, Buyer desires to purchase from Seller and Seller desires to sell to Buyer all of the Member Interests on the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Agreement.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual agreements, representations, warranties and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, and subject to the conditions contained herein, the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
