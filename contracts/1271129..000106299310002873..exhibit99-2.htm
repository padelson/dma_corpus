WHEREAS:

A.

Acquiror wishes to acquire all of the issued and outstanding common shares of Target;

   
B

Acquiror, Target and Spinco wish to propose an arrangement involving, among other things, a reorganization of the capital of Target, the distribution to Target Shareholders of 90.1% of the outstanding Spinco Shares and the acquisition by Acquiror of all of the issued and outstanding common shares of Target in exchange for common shares of Acquiror and cash;

D.

E.

The Parties have entered into this Agreement to provide for the matters referred to in the foregoing recitals and for other matters relating to such arrangement.
