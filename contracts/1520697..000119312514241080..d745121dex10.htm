1. Issuance of Notes. Subject to the terms and conditions herein contained, the Company proposes to issue and sell to the several initial purchasers named in Schedule I hereto (the “Initial Purchasers”), $300,000,000 aggregate principal amount of the Company’s 5.125% Senior Notes due 2022 (each a “Note” and, collectively, the “Notes”) in connection with the offering and sale of the Notes as described herein (the “Offering”).
