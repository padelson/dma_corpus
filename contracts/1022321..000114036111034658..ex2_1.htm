INTRODUCTION 1. The Sellers and their affiliates, collectively, have developed a private black oil marine barge transportation business, which includes 30 barges (seven of which are leased) and 14 push boats; 2. The Buyer and its affiliates have developed a marine barge transportation business; 3. The Sellers desire to sell their black oil marine barge transportation business, and the Buyer desires to purchase same, upon the terms and conditions set forth herein; 4. Although PBC does not own any of the assets to be sold and acquired, it is the personnel staffing affiliate to the other Sellers and, thus, is a party to this agreement.
