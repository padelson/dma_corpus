AGREEMENT AND PLAN OF MERGER
     THIS AGREEMENT AND PLAN OF MERGER (this “Agreement”) is made and entered into as of April 17, 2010, by and among Hospira, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Parent”), Discus Acquisition Corporation, a Delaware corporation and a wholly owned Subsidiary of Parent (“Merger Sub” and, together with Parent, the “Buyer Parties”), and Javelin Pharmaceuticals, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the “Company”).
RECITALS
     WHEREAS, the respective boards of directors of Parent, Merger Sub and the Company have determined by the requisite majority that it is in the best interests of their respective stockholders for Parent to acquire the Company upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth herein (the “Acquisition”);
     WHEREAS, contemporaneously with the execution and delivery of this Agreement, the Company has terminated, in accordance with its terms, that certain Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated as of December 18, 2009, by and among Myriad Pharmaceuticals, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Myriad”), MPI Merger Sub, Inc., a Delaware corporation, the Company and Frederick E. Pierce, II, as representative of the Company’s stockholders (the “Myriad Merger Agreement”);
     WHEREAS, on or before the execution and delivery of this Agreement, Parent and the Company are entering into a loan and security agreement (the “Loan Agreement”) in substantially the form attached hereto as Exhibit B, pursuant to which the Company is issuing a secured promissory note (the “Note”) to Parent, pursuant to which Parent may lend up to $17,234,767.19 to the Company in accordance with the terms set forth in the Loan Agreement, the Note, an intellectual property security agreement and other related documents (collectively, the “Note Documents”);
     WHEREAS, in furtherance of the Acquisition, Parent proposes to cause Merger Sub to make a tender offer (as it may be amended from time to time as permitted under this Agreement, the “Offer”) to purchase all of the issued and outstanding shares of common stock, par value $0.001 per share, of the Company (the “Company Common Stock”), for $2.20 per share (such amount, or any greater amount per share paid pursuant to the Offer, being the “Offer Price”), net to the stockholders of the Company (the “Company Stockholders”) in cash, without interest, upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Agreement and the Offer;
     WHEREAS, the board of directors of the Company (the “Company Board”) has approved the making of the Offer and resolved to recommend that the Company Stockholders tender their shares of Company Common Stock pursuant to the Offer;
     WHEREAS, also in furtherance of the Acquisition, the boards of directors of the Company, Parent and Merger Sub have each approved this Agreement by the requisite majority and declared its advisability and approved the merger of Merger Sub with and into the Company (the “Merger”) in accordance with the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (the
