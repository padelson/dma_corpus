(b) The final amount of the consideration payable to the Sellers (the " _Final Purchase Price_ ") shall be equal to the Provisional Purchase Price increased or decreased, as applicable, by the Working Capital Adjustment, the Cash Balances Adjustment, the Indebtedness Adjustment, the Transaction Expenses Adjustment and the Change of Control Payments Adjustment, as finally determined pursuant to _Section _ _2.03_. 2.03 _Determination of Final Purchase Price_. The Closing Accounts and the Adjustment Statement shall be prepared in good faith and in accordance with the Accounting Principles.
