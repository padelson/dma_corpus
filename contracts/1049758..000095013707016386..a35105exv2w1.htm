The Merger Consideration shall be adjusted appropriately to reflect the effect of any stock split, reverse stock split, ##### stock dividend (including any dividend or distribution of securities convertible into Common Stock), cash dividend, reorganization, recapitalization, reclassification, combination, exchange of shares or other like change with respect to Common Stock occurring on or after the date hereof and prior to the Effective Time.
