At the Effective Time, holders of LSBG Common Stock shall cease to be, and shall have no rights as, shareholders of LSBG other than the right to receive the Merger Consideration provided under this Article II. No such holder shall be entitled to dividends, voting rights or any other rights in respect of any fractional share. _Section 2.04_ _Treasury Shares; Shares Owned by the Parties_.
