All fees and expenses relating to the work, if any, to be performed by the Independent Accounting Firm pursuant to this Section 2.2 will be allocated between Seller and Buyer in inverse proportion as each shall prevail in respect of the dollar amount of disputed items so submitted (as finally determined by the Independent Accounting Firm).
