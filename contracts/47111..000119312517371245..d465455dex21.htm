C. As soon as practicable following the consummation of the Offer, upon the terms and conditions set forth herein and in accordance with the General Corporation Law of the State of Delaware (the "DGCL"), Acquisition Sub will be merged with and into the Company (the "Merger") with the Company as the surviving corporation (the "Surviving Corporation"), whereby each share (except as otherwise provided herein) of Company Common Stock not owned directly or indirectly by Parent, Acquisition Sub or the Company will be converted into the right to receive the Offer Price, net to the seller in cash, without interest and reduced by the amount of any withholding that is required under applicable Tax Law, in accordance with _Section_ __ _ 3.11_, upon the terms and subject to the conditions of this Agreement.
