    BACKGROUND
 
A.   The Seller is the sole shareholder of Sefina Finance AB, Reg. No. 556747-0710, a limited liability company incorporated under the laws of Sweden (the “Company”).
 
B.   The Company and its Subsidiaries (as defined below) are engaged in the business of pawnbroking and other ancillary related services in Sweden and Finland. C.   The Seller wishes to sell the Shares (as defined below) to the Buyer, and the Buyer wishes to buy the Shares from the Seller.
 
D.   In view of the foregoing, the Parties have entered into this Agreement.
