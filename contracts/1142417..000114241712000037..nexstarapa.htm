ASSET PURCHASE AGREEMENT
 
THIS ASSET PURCHASE AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) is made as of July 18, 2012, by and among (i) Newport Television LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“Newport”), Newport Television License LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“Newport License,” and together with Newport, “Seller” and each, a “Seller”) and (ii) Nexstar Broadcasting, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Buyer”).
 
Recitals
 
A.           Newport and High Plains Broadcasting Operating Company LLC (“High Plains”)  are the owners of the assets (other than the FCC Licenses) used in the operation of the television broadcast stations identified on Exhibit A hereto (each, a “Station” and collectively, the “Stations”) and operate such Stations pursuant to certain authorizations issued by the Federal Communications Commission (the “FCC”).
 
B.           Newport and High Plains are engaged in the business and operation of the Stations (the “Stations Business”) and the business of producing, marketing, promoting, selling, licensing, distributing or otherwise commercializing integrated digital management software, systems, solutions or services for local media companies on-air, online and on mobile devices (the “Inergize Business”) (collectively, the “Business” which, for the avoidance of doubt, shall not include the Other Seller Stations (as defined below) or the other businesses or assets of Seller or High Plains).
 
C.           The FCC Licenses are held by Newport License and High Plains Broadcasting License Company LLC (“High Plains License”) (collectively, the “FCC Licensees”).
 
D.           Newport has exercised its option to purchase from High Plains and High Plains License (collectively, the “High Plains Entities” and each, a “High Plains Entity”) substantially all of the assets (including the FCC Licenses) owned by the High Plains Entities and used in the operation of KUCW(TV), Ogden, Utah (the “High Plains Purchased Assets”) and assume certain liabilities of the High Plains Entities (the “High Plains Assumed Liabilities”) pursuant to that certain Option Exercise Agreement dated July 18, 2012 by and among Newport, High Plains and High Plains License (the “Option Exercise Agreement”).
 
E.           Pursuant to the terms of the Option Exercise Agreement, the High Plains Entities have agreed to take all action reasonably necessary or required by Seller to facilitate the sale and transfer of the High Plains Purchased Assets by Seller to any Person designated by Seller in writing.
 
F.           The closing of the transactions contemplated by the Option Exercise Agreement and the closing of the transactions contemplated by this Agreement shall occur simultaneously and, at such closings, Seller will, and, pursuant to the terms of the Option Exercise Agreement, Seller will cause and direct the High Plains Entities to, assign and transfer to Buyer, and Buyer will purchase and assume, the High Plains Purchased Assets and the High Plains Assumed Liabilities.
 
G.           Pursuant to the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Agreement, Seller desires to sell to Buyer and to cause the High Plains Entities to seler Buyer, and Buyer desires to purchase from Seller and from the High Plains Entities, the Purchased Assets (as defined below).
