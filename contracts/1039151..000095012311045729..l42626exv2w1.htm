Each share of class A common stock, par value $0.01 per share, of Merger Sub issued and outstanding immediately prior to the Effective Time shall be, without any further action on the part of the shareholders of Merger Sub, converted into and become one validly issued, fully paid and non-assessable share of class A common stock, par value $0.01 per share, of the Surviving Entity (the " _Survivor Class A Common Stock_").
