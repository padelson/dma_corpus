The foregoing shall not in any way limit or qualify any of the representations, warranties, covenants and agreements of the Seller hereunder. The Seller desires to sell, and Purchaser desires to purchase, all of the issued and outstanding shares of capital stock of the Company (the " _Shares_ ") on the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Agreement.
