This Unit Purchase Agreement (this “Agreement”), dated as of May 4, 2016, is entered into between Ennis, Inc., a Texas corporation (“Seller”), and Gildan Activewear Inc., a Canadian corporation (“Buyer”).

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Seller owns 100 units, constituting all of the limited liability company interests (the “Units”), of Alstyle Apparel, LLC, a Delaware Limited Liability Company (the “Company”);

WHEREAS, Seller owns the assets set forth on Exhibit A to this Agreement (the “Acquired Assets”);

WHEREAS, Seller wishes to sell to Buyer, and Buyer wishes to purchase from Seller, the Units and the Acquired Assets, subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein; and

WHEREAS, Buyer and Seller have entered into or shall enter into, as applicable, the Escrow Agreements (as defined herein), pursuant to which Buyer shall deposit $5,000,000 in the aggregate (the “Escrow Amount”), which amount shall be credited against the Base Amount as set forth in Section 2.02.
