Each share of Socrata Stock that is directly owned by Socrata, as treasury stock, shall automatically be canceled at the Effective Time and shall cease to exist, and no consideration shall be delivered in exchange therefor. Each share of Series C Preferred Stock issued and outstanding immediately prior to the Effective Time (other than (i) shares of Series C Preferred Stock to be cancelled in accordance with Section 3.1(a), and (ii) shares of Series C Preferred Stock that are Dissenting Shares) shall be converted into the right to receive (without interest) an amount in cash equal to $2.9017 (the "Series C Per Share Merger Consideration"), which shall be payable in accordance with Section 3.4.
