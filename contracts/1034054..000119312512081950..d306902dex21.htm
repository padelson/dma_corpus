WHEREAS, prior to the date of this Agreement: (a) Mobilitie Holdings I, Mobilitie Partners I and Mobilitie LLC formed New Mobilitie Investments, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“New M1”); (b) Mobilitie Holdings II, Mobilitie Partners II and Mobilitie LLC formed New Mobilitie Investments II, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“New M2”); (c) Mobilitie I formed MPGJ I and MPMA I; and (d) Mobilitie II formed MPGJ II;
