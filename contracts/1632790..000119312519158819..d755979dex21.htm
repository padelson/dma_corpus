ACQUISITION AGREEMENT

This ACQUISITION AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) is dated as of May 29, 2019, between Energizer Holdings, Inc., a Missouri corporation (“Seller”), and VARTA Aktiengesellschaft, a stock corporation under German law listed on the regulated market of the Frankfurt stock exchange (“Purchaser”).

W I T N E S S E T H:

WHEREAS, Seller beneficially owns and operates (indirectly through the Transferred Entities) the Business operated by the Transferred Entities;

WHEREAS, Seller wishes to sell and Purchaser wishes to acquire the Transferred Entities and the Business operated by the Transferred Entities on the terms and subject to the conditions contained in this Agreement;

WHEREAS, the wholly owned Subsidiaries of Seller listed as Subsidiary Transferors on Exhibit A-1 are the direct owners of the equity interests in each of the entities set forth opposite such Person’s name in Exhibit A-1 (such entities as of the Closing Date are collectively referred to as the “Varta Companies” and such equity interests are collectively referred to as the “Varta Companies Equity Interests”);

WHEREAS, the applicable Varta Company is (or will at the Closing be) the direct or indirect owner of all the equity interests in each of the entities listed below such Varta Company’s name in Exhibit A-2 (such entities as of the Closing Date are collectively referred to as the “Varta Company Subsidiaries” and such equity interests are collectively referred to as the “Varta Company Subsidiaries’ Equity Interests”; the Varta Companies and the Varta Company Subsidiaries are collectively referred to as the “Transferred Entities”; the Varta Companies Equity Interests and the Varta Company Subsidiaries’ Equity Interests are collectively referred to as the “Transferred Equity Interests”);

WHEREAS, at the Closing, upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Agreement, Seller and the Subsidiary Transferors desire to sell and transfer to Purchaser or its designees, and Purchaser or its designees desire to purchase and accept and assume from Seller and the Subsidiary Transferors, the Varta Companies Equity Interests, and thereby indirectly the Transferred Assets and the Assumed Liabilities (the “Acquisition”); and

WHEREAS, after giving effect to the Pre-Closing Restructuring, immediately after the Closing, Purchaser or its designees shall own and be responsible for the Business by (a) directly owning the Varta Companies Equity Interests, (b) indirectly owning the Varta Company Subsidiaries’ Equity Interests, and (c) indirectly owning the Transferred Assets held by the Transferred Entities and indirectly being responsible for the Assumed Liabilities of the Transferred Entities.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the premises and the mutual representations, warranties, covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:
