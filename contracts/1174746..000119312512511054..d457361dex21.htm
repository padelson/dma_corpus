Upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Agreement and in accordance with the Delaware General Corporation Law, as amended (the " _DGCL_ "), and the Delaware Limited Liability Company Act, as amended (the " _DLLCA_ "), at the Effective Time, the Merger shall occur pursuant to which Yankees shall merge with and into Merger Sub, with Merger Sub surviving the Merger (the " _Surviving Company_ "), and the separate corporate existence of Yankees shall thereupon cease.
