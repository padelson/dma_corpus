(b) At and effective as of the Closing, the Purchaser shall assume, agree to pay, perform, fulfill and discharge when due in accordance with their terms the Assumed Liabilities. Section 1.02 _Purchase Price_. The aggregate consideration to be paid by the Purchaser to the Seller for the Transferred Equity Interests and the Transferred Assets shall be (a) an amount in cash equal to Eight Hundred Fifty Million Dollars ($850,000,000) (the " _Base Purchase Price_ "), which amount shall be adjusted as a result of the Closing Adjustment, if any, pursuant to Section 1.04, and the Post-Closing Adjustment, if any, pursuant to Section 1.10 (such Base Purchase Price, as so adjusted, the " _Purchase Price_ ") and _plus_ (b) the assumption of the Assumed Liabilities.
