STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT

Recitals
 
A.           The Sellers own all of the issued and outstanding shares of WinDoor’s common stock, par value $0.01 (the “Shares”), and all of the issued and outstanding membership units  of LTE (the “Units”).
 
B.           The Sellers wish to sell to Buyer, and Buyer wishes to purchase from the Sellers, the Shares and the Units for the Purchase Price and upon the terms and subject to the conditions hereinafter set forth (together with the other transactions contemplated by this Agreement, the “Transaction”).
