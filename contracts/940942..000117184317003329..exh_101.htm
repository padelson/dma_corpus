RECITALS:

 

A.       Seller is engaged in the transportation of goods in interstate and intrastate commerce as a common carrier and contract carrier (the “Business”).

 

B.       The Equityholders own, beneficially and of record, all of the outstanding limited liability membership interests of Seller.
