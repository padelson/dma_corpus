PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

 

This PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”), dated as of November 19, 2020 (the “Effective Date”) is made and entered into by and between NRG Solar Sunrise LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“Seller”), and Clearway AC Solar Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“Purchaser”). Seller and Purchaser are referred to, collectively, as the “Parties” and each, individually, as a “Party.” Capitalized terms used herein shall have the meanings set forth in Section 1.01.

 

RECITALS

 

WHEREAS, Seller owns one hundred percent (100%) of the outstanding membership interests of Agua Caliente Borrower 1 LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (the “Company”);

 

WHEREAS, the Company owns thirty five percent (35%) of the membership interests of AC Solar Holdings LLC (“AC Holdings”), which in turn owns all of the membership interests of Agua Caliente Solar Holdings LLC (“Agua Holdings”), which in turn owns all of the membership interests of Agua Caliente Solar LLC (“AC Solar”, and together with AC Holdings and Agua Holdings, the “Subsidiaries”); and

 

WHEREAS, Seller desires to sell, and Purchaser desires to purchase, one hundred percent (100%) of the outstanding membership interests of the Company (the “Acquired Interests”) but not including the Excluded Assets (as defined herein), on the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Agreement.
