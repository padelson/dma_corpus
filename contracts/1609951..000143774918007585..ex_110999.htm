This Agreement provides for the acquisition of Landmark by NCC pursuant to the merger of Landmark with and into NCC (the " **Merger** "). At the effective time of the Merger, and except as provided herein, the outstanding shares of capital stock of Landmark shall be converted into the right to receive a fixed number of shares of common stock of NCC and a fixed amount of cash. As a result, stockholders of Landmark shall become stockholders of NCC. The transactions described in or otherwise contemplated by this Agreement are subject to, among other things: (i) the filing by NCC and the effectiveness of a registration statement with respect to the shares of common stock of NCC to be issued to stockholders of Landmark in the Merger; (ii) the approval of the stockholders of Landmark; (iii) the approval of, notice to and/or waiver of the Federal Reserve, the OCC and the Georgia Department of Banking and Finance; and (iv) the satisfaction of certain other conditions described in this Agreement.
