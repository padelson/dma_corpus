Upon the terms and subject to the conditions of this Agreement, at the Closing, Seller agrees to, and agrees to cause the Seller Affiliates to, sell, transfer, assign and deliver to Purchaser, and Purchaser agrees to purchase, acquire and accept from Seller and the Seller Affiliates, all of Sellers and the Seller Affiliates right, title and interest in, to and under the Transferred Assets as of the Closing for (i) an aggregate purchase price of $580,000,000 (the " _Purchase Price_ "), payable as set forth in Section 2.02(b)(i) and subject to adjustment as set forth in Section 2.03, and (ii) the assumption of the Assumed Liabilities.
