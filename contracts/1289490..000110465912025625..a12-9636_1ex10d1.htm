MEMBERSHIP INTEREST PURCHASE AGREEMENT

RECITALS

 

A.                                    ESS and PRIII Member are the sole members of ESS PRISA III LLC (the “JV”).

 

B.                                    ESS desires to acquire 100% of PRIII Member’s interest in the JV (the “Interests”), such Interests to be free and clear of any debt, encumbrances or adverse claims.

 

C.                                    As a result of the foregoing, immediately following the Closing, ESS will own 100% of the Interests.
