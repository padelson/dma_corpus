STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT

WHEREAS, the Shareholder is the record and beneficial owner of all of the issued and outstanding shares (the “Shares”) of the capital stock of Simonton; and
WHEREAS, the Shareholder desires to sell, and Buyer desires to purchase, the Shares owned by the Shareholder, upon the terms and conditions set forth herein.
