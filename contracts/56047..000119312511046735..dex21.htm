PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT

This PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT, dated as of February 21, 2011 (this “Agreement”) is made and entered into by and between United Engines Holding Company, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“Seller”) and Kirby Engine Systems, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Buyer”).

RECITALS

Seller desires to sell to Buyer, and Buyer desires to purchase from Seller, all interests in United Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (“United Holdings”) on the terms and subject to the conditions set forth herein.

AGREEMENT

Now, therefore, in consideration of the premises and the mutual representations, warranties, covenants and agreements in this Agreement, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, and intending to be legally bound hereby, the Parties hereby agree as follows:
