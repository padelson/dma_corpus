Introduction
 
The Buyer wishes to purchase from the Sellers, and the Sellers wish to sell to the Buyer, all of the outstanding capital stock of Tube Supply, Inc., a Texas corporation and wholly-owned by the Sellers (the “Company”), on the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement.
 
An index of defined terms is set forth in Section 9.13.
