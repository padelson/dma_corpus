THIS PURCHASE AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) is made and entered into as of the 20th day of October 2011 by and among Teleflex Incorporated, a Delaware corporation (“Seller”), AAR International, Inc., an Illinois corporation (“Buyer”) and, solely for purposes of Article IX, AAR CORP., a Delaware corporation.

 

BACKGROUND

 

A.            Seller owns and operates, through direct and indirect subsidiaries, the Business.

 

B.            Seller and the Selling Subsidiaries desire to sell, transfer and assign to Buyer, and Buyer desires to purchase from Seller and the Selling Subsidiaries, the Acquired Companies and the Air Cargo Assets, and Buyer desires to assume the Assumed Liabilities, as more specifically provided herein.
