STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT
 
This Stock Purchase Agreement (this “Agreement”) is made and dated as of August 17, 2011, by and among Cree, Inc., a North Carolina corporation (the “Buyer”); those individuals and entities set forth on Schedule A (the “Sellers”), who are all of the shareholders of Ruud Lighting, Inc., a Wisconsin corporation (the “Company”); Christopher A. The Sellers desire to sell, and the Buyer desires to purchase, all of the issued and outstanding shares of capital stock of the Company for the Purchase Price and on the terms set forth in this Agreement.
