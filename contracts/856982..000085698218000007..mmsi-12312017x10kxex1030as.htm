The fees and expenses of the Accounting Mediator will be shared equally by Seller and Purchaser, and the decision of the Accounting Mediator with respect to the items of the Final Calculation Statements submitted to it will be final, conclusive and binding on the parties. For purposes of complying with this Section 2.2, Purchaser and Seller will be afforded the opportunity to present to the Accounting Mediator any material related to the Objections and to discuss the items with the Accounting Mediator. (iii) Amount. The Purchase Price Adjustment (which may be positive, negative or zero) shall be calculated as an amount equal to the Closing Inventory minus the Estimated Inventory.
