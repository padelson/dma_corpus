Seller and Buyer are sometimes referred to herein individually as a “Party” and collectively as the “Parties.”

Seller owns all of the issued and outstanding member interests (the “Interests”) of Hilcorp Energy GOM, LLC, a Texas limited liability company (the “Company”), and Seller desires to sell to Buyer, and Buyer desires to purchase from Seller, the Interests, on and subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
