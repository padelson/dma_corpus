AGREEMENT FOR SHARE EXCHANGE


THIS AGREEMENT FOR SHARE EXCHANGE (this “Agreement”) is dated as of the 29th day of August 2007, by and among China RuiTai International Holdings Co., Ltd., a Delaware corporation (“China RuiTai”), and Pacific Capital Group Co. Ltd., a Republic of Vanuatu corporation (“Pacific Capital Group”), and the Shareholders of Pacific Capital Group listed in Exhibit A who execute this Agreement (the “Shareholders”) (collectively referred to as the “Parties”).


RECITALS


WHEREAS, China RuiTai and Pacific Capital Group desire to complete a share exchange transaction pursuant to which China RuiTai shall acquire all of the issued and outstanding common stock of Pacific Capital Group in exchange for the issuance of 22,645,348 shares of restricted common stock of China RuiTai to the Shareholders; and


WHEREAS, the Board of Directors of China RuiTai and the Board of Directors of Pacific Capital Group have each approved the proposed transaction, contingent upon satisfaction prior to closing of all of the terms and conditions of this Agreement; and


WHEREAS, the Shareholders are the owners of all of the issued and outstanding common stock of Pacific Capital Group; and


WHEREAS, the Parties desire to make certain representations, warranties and agreements in connection with completion of the proposed share exchange transaction.


NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the foregoing recitals, which shall be considered an integral part of this Agreement, and the covenants, conditions, representations and

warranties hereinafter set forth, the Parties hereby agree as follows:


ARTICLE I

THE EXCHANGE


1.1

The Exchange.  At the Closing (as hereinafter defined), China RuiTai shall acquire all of the issued and outstanding common stock of Pacific Capital Group from the Shareholders. Consideration to be issued by China RuiTai shall be a total of 22,645,348 restricted shares of its common stock (the “Exchange Shares”) in exchange for 100% of the issued and outstanding common stock of Pacific Capital Group (the “Exchange”).  The Exchange shall take place upon the terms and conditions provided for in this Agreement and in accordance with applicable law. Immediately following completion of the share exchange transaction through issuance of the Exchange Shares, China RuiTai shall have a total of approximately 26,000,000 shares of its common stock issued and outstanding.
