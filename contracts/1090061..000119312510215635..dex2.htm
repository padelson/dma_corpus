Dear Sirs:

Following our recent discussions in relation to the contemplated acquisition of all the outstanding shares and other securities (the “Securities”) representing 100 % of the share capital and voting rights (on a fully diluted basis) of Eliokem International, a French société par actions simplifiée with a share capital of € 15,300,000 whose registered office is at ZA de Courtaboeuf 2 – 14 avenue des Tropiques, 91955 Villejust, with identification number 491 529 673 RCS Evry (the “Company”), Omnova Solutions Inc hereby submit the following irrevocable and binding offer (the “Put”) which AXA Investment Managers Private Equity Europe (“AXA”), acting as (i) société de gestion for AXA LBO Fund III-A and AXA LBO Fund III-B and (ii) Sellers’ Agent, may, at its sole option, elect to accept in accordance with the procedures and subject to the terms set out below, and whereby Omnova Solutions Inc (or any of its Affiliates) (the “Purchaser”), will, in accordance with, and subject to, the terms and conditions of the share purchase agreement (together with its own schedules, appendices and exhibits) attached hereto as Exhibit 1 (the “Share Purchase Agreement”), acquire all, and only all, of the Securities from the Sellers.

Capitalized terms used in this letter (the “Letter”) shall, unless otherwise defined herein, have the meanings ascribed to them in the Share Purchase Agreement.

 

1. Duration and acceptance of the Put

The Put will remain valid until the earlier of (i) 8:00 pm, Paris time, the fifth Business Day after the date on which the central worker’s council (comité central d’entreprise) of the Group Companies, following its information and consultation, will have delivered its final opinion in respect of the Transaction, and (ii) 8:00 pm, Paris time, December 20, 2010 (the “Expiry Date”).

The Put will automatically terminate as at the Expiry Date, unless the Parties mutually agree to postpone such date to a later date.

This Put may be exercised and accepted by AXA in accordance with the provisions of Article 3, on or prior to the Expiry Date.

 

2. Information and consultation of the central worker’s council

Purchaser acknowledges that the contemplated acquisition of the Company by the Purchaser is subject to the central worker’s council (comité central d’entreprise) of the Group Companies being informed and consulted on the Transaction and delivering a final opinion on such Transaction.

By countersigning this letter, AXA undertakes to (i) cause the management of the Group Companies to inform and consult the central worker’s council of the Group Companies on the proposed Transaction as soon as practicable after the date hereof and (ii) to regularly inform the Purchaser of the progress of the information and consultation.

In this respect, Purchaser undertakes to co-operate, as may be reasonably required by AXA, with AXA and the managers of the Group Companies, with respect to the information and consultation of the central worker’s council (including by providing the documents or information relating to the Purchaser and its affiliates or its strategy relating to the Group Companies, which the central worker’s council or the “expert” appointed by the central worker’s council, as the case may be, would reasonably request in connection only with the contemplated change of control of the Group Companies and to extent required by applicable laws).

From the date hereof and until execution of the Share Purchase Agreement, AXA and the Sellers having countersigned this Letter will not be committed to sell the Securities to the Purchaser and will not be bound by any obligation in connection with this Transaction, with the exception of (i) all covenants of Article 6 of the Share Purchase Agreement, (ii) the exclusivity undertaking provided for under Clause 6 (“Exclusivity”) of this Letter and (iii) the other undertakings expressly provided for in this Letter.

 

3. Exercise of the Put - execution of the Share Purchase Agreement

If AXA decides to accept and exercise the Put, it shall do so by sending to Purchaser one copy of the Share Purchase Agreement executed by all the Sellers before the Expiry Date.

Subject to the fulfillment of the Condition Precedent set out in Clause 5 below, if AXA exercises the Put as set forth above, Purchaser hereby irrevocably and unconditionally undertakes to execute the Share Purchase Agreement within three (3) Business Days following the date of receipt by Purchaser of the Share Purchase Agreement executed by the Sellers, it being provided that any revocation from Purchaser of the Put before that date will be without effect.

 

4. Antitrust Clearance

To the extent permissible, Purchaser undertakes to initiate all necessary filings, with the Required Antitrust Authorities as soon as reasonably possible as from the date hereof.

By countersigning this Letter, AXA undertakes to fully cooperate and to cause the Group Companies to cooperate with the Purchaser, in a timely fashion, in providing such assistance as is reasonably necessary for the Purchaser to make such filings.

5. Condition Precedent

Purchaser’s undertaking set out in Clause 3 above is subject to the non occurrence, before the execution of the Share Purchase Agreement, of any situation, transaction, development, event, circumstance, change, occurrence, effect or fact that would prevent any of the conditions precedent referred to in Article 5 of the Share Purchase Agreement (except for the financing condition precedent referred to in Article 5.3.1) to be satisfied (the “Conditions Precedent”). For the sake of clarity, the Conditions Precedent are intended for the exclusive benefit of the Purchaser and the Purchaser may, at any time, waive by notice to AXA, the benefit of it.

 

6. Exclusivity

In consideration for Purchaser’s irrevocable commitment hereunder, AXA agree that, as from the date hereof and until December 31, 2010 included, AXA and the Sellers (or any of their Affiliates, including any Group Companies) shall not, directly or indirectly, continue or enter into any discussions or negotiations with, or provide information to, any third party and/or enter into any agreement, in both cases, for or in connection with the transfer, by any means, of all or part of the Securities or any securities of the Group Companies or substantial assets of the Group Companies, or the merger, spin-off, contribution, business combination, or similar transaction involving the Group Companies, other than with the Purchaser.

 

7. Deloitte’s fees

We undertake to bear the Deloitte’s Fees (including, for the avoidance of doubt, if the Condition Precedent is not satisfied), except if AXA decides not to exercise the Put or if the non-satisfaction of the Condition Precedent is a consequence of AXA’s fault or breach of any of its obligations hereunder.

 

8. Governing law

This Letter shall be exclusively governed by, construed in accordance with, and enforced under, French law and all disputes, controversies or claims arising out of, or in connection with, the existence, validity, interpretation or performance of this letter shall be brought before the Tribunal de Commerce of Paris.
